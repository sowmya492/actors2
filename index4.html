Pawan Kalyan is an Indian actor, politician, filmmaker, martial artist, and philanthropist who primarily works in Telugu cinema.
Known for his unique acting style and mannerisms, he has a large fan base and a cult following.
Born: 2 September 1971 (age 52 years), Bapatla.
Spouse: Anna Lezhneva (m. 2013), Renu Desai (m. 2009–2012), Nandini (m. 1997–2008)
Children: Akira Nandan
Party: JanaSena Party
